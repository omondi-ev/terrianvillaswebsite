Welcome to Terrian Villas

Luxury Living Along Nairobi-Namanga Highway

Exclusive Townhouses with Modern Amenities

Terrian Villas presents a unique residential community featuring premium 3, 4, and 5 bedroom townhouses, each with a DSQ (Domestic Staff Quarter). Nestled along the prestigious Nairobi-Namanga Highway, our development offers the perfect blend of luxury living and convenience.

Phase 1: Sports & Commercial Complex

Front section featuring comprehensive sports facilities including football field, basketball court, indoor games arena, and vibrant commercial area with shopping mall.

Phase 2: Residential Sanctuary

Behind the sports complex lies our exclusive residential community, offering privacy and tranquility with beautifully designed townhouses.
